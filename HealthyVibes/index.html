✨ About HealthyVibes

“HealthyVibes” is a fresh, vibrant TikTok community dedicated to skin health, wellness, and inner beauty. Managed by Miss Eiyla, an experienced analyst for multiple girl-based TikTok teams including Siskavera, Bunga Kelate, and others. The core team members currently consist of Siska, Aina, and newcomer Wawa.

Their honest product reviews, interactive live-streams, and educational content have attracted a loyal base of skincare lovers and health-conscious fans. HealthyVibes is trusted by viewers looking for real-time feedback and results from products they’re curious to try.

As of now, the account has successfully grown to 2447 followers and 2161 likes, with their top-performing video reaching over 20.5K views. They actively partner with wellness brands and cosmetics suppliers to bring firsthand insights and test kits to their growing audience.

Among the brand collaborations are successful campaigns with PA!, Peterson's Lab, HFP, and many other reputable cosmetic and wellness labels, strengthening their credibility in the industry.
