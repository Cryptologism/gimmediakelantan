🌟 About Garage Kelate

Garage Kelate is a dynamic TikTok account managed by Kall, Imran, Alia, Aina, and Chuu, focusing on promoting a wide range of automotive products and accessories. Through engaging live streams, they offer product showcases, demonstrations, and exclusive deals to their audience.

With over 18.1K followers and 15.1K likes, Garage Kelate has built a loyal automotive community that relies on them for quality products and practical demonstrations.

🚗 Featured Categories

  • Automotive Accessories – Sunshades, organizers, dashcams, and more.
  • Car Maintenance Products – Battery jumpers, chargers, cleaning kits.
  • Live Demonstrations – Real-time tests and feedback from viewers.
