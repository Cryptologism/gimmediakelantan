🌟 About Akak Kelate

“Akak Kelate” is a leading kitchenware-focused TikTok creator known for honest reviews, live demos, and engaging home cooking content. Managed by Shahrul Yoe and team under @akak.kelate, they have built a strong reputation for helping families choose the right products for their kitchens.

With 99.2K followers and over 519.3K likes, Akak Kelate has become a household name among Malaysian homemakers. Their real-life testing and cooking shows create trust among buyers, especially for trending kitchen gadgets and appliances.

🤝 Brand Collaborations

Akak Kelate has proudly partnered with top kitchenware brands including:

  • HULMERS – Known for quality pots and pans. Akak Kelate cooked rendang using their ceramic cookware live.
  • DESSINI – Frying pans and dual grill pans loved by their followers. Many sold out during livestreams.
  • SIMPLUS – From air fryers to multifunctional rice cookers, Simplus is a top pick for modern kitchen needs.
  • SWISSTHOMAS – Their pressure cookers and gas stoves often featured in cooking demo lives.
  • GAABOR – Air fryer brand trusted for its safety and consistent results. Fans love the compact design.

  • LQLQ – Affordable tools and gadgets like garlic choppers and oil sprayers. Viral among kitchen moms.
  • MUGEN – Stylish, minimalistic electric stoves and mini ovens that Akak Kelate shows off for urban homes.

Their success lies in one simple formula — “kami guna, kami tunjuk live, kami jujur.” That’s what makes the audience trust them.
